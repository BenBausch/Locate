About Us!

Welcome to Locate, your go-to platform for discovering and sharing sport infrastructure locations on OpenStreetMap. We are passionate about helping sports enthusiasts find the perfect venues for their favorite activities, whether it's basketball, golf, tennis, and so much more.

At Locate, we understand the importance of having convenient access to well-maintained sports facilities. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to engage in their preferred sports and stay active, regardless of their location. That's why we've built a community-driven platform that empowers users like you to contribute to the mapping of sport infrastructure worldwide.

Our platform allows users to add sport infrastructure locations directly to OpenStreetMap, a collaborative mapping project that relies on the input of volunteers. By contributing your local knowledge and adding new venues, you're helping to create a comprehensive and up-to-date database of sports facilities that can benefit the entire community.

In addition to adding new locations, Locate also provides you with a convenient way to explore all the sport infrastructure that other users have contributed. With our user-friendly interface, you can search for specific sports or browse through various categories to find the perfect spot for your favorite activities. Whether you're looking for a basketball court in your neighborhood or a golf course in a new city, Locate has you covered.

We strive to make your experience on Locate seamless and enjoyable. Our platform is designed to provide you with accurate and detailed information about each sports facility, including its amenities, opening hours, and user reviews. We believe in the power of community-driven information sharing, as it enables you to make informed decisions and find the best venues that suit your needs.

Join the Locate community today and be a part of our mission to create a comprehensive and accessible database of sport infrastructure locations worldwide. Together, we can make it easier for sports enthusiasts like you to discover new places, stay active, and pursue their passion for various sports.

Get started with Locate and unlock a world of sports possibilities. Let's play, explore, and connect through sports!
